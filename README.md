# EOA HP/Multiverse Portfolio Project

## About Me:
My name is Damien Hall, I am a Software Developer by trade and by hobby! Ever since I was little I have always enjoyed computers, gaming, and programming. I started my programming journy around 7 when my father showed me how we automated a part of his job using Visual Basic Script. Excited about the possibilities, this got me into trying to automate parts of my life like him. I wrote algorithms to solve crossword puzzles, custom flashcard web-apps to help me remember Ionic Compounds and even tried to make some games!

After  writing programs for so many year, I naturally took any tech-related job that I could find. I started my career during my associates degree by getting a job at the Innovation Lab at my college. This maker space allowed me to meet many people including proffessors and  other people who helped me take the next steps in my life. The Innovation Lab allowed me to meet the colleges programming proffessor long before I was  set to due to course requirements. This connection, along with some good impressions and a showcase of my skillset set me up for a job at Espresso Moon, a custom software development startup with a subsidary company GageList.

After a year of work in the startup I learned many skills, all of which I added to my LinkedIn, allowing me to get noticed by a recruiter for Multiverse! That connection led me to my 15 month apprenticeship with Multiverse and HP.

## Apprenticeship Overview:
- **Host Company:** HP
- **Duration:** 15 months working on course work assigned by Multiverse and 12 months on the job training at HP in tandem with that Multiverse work.

## Project Overview:
This project is a portfolio of work completed throughout the HP / Multiverse apprenticeship. It includes all projects that have been completed during the following Multiverse modules:
1. SWE Front End Module
2. SWE Back End Module
3. SWE Deployment Module
4. Hackathon Module
5. SWE Cyber Security Module

>__Note__: This doesn't include all work completed during the apprenticeship, but only work that showcases multiple necessary skills to complete the apprenticeship.

On top of these projects, I will also include other sections in this portfolio that include other projects I've completed outside of this apprenticeship and other relevant work experience.
